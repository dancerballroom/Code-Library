  • How to practice
    • Practice Keeping Time
      • Play at a Slow at a perfect Metronome marking (no slowing down or speeding up)
      • Increase the Metronome rate and maintain no slowing down or speeding up.
    • Practice Jumping out and back in with the Time of the Music
      • Leave out a measure or a couple of notes keeping track of the timing.
      • (This is good practice for playing with other instruments or voices)
    • Practice Reading ahead
      • Memorize Hard passages.
      • (Read ahead to handle rough passages and page turns)
    • Practice Transposing
      • Add a Sharp to the Signature,  Key of C to Key of G
      • Add a Flat to the Signature:  Key of C to Key of F
    • Practice Playing for the best Sound
      • Dynamic Range (Quiet to Loud)
      • Expression
      • Voicing
    • Practice Playing Chords and Singing the Melody
      • Keeping the timing accurate.
      • Emphasize he natural Breathing Pauses/Phrases
