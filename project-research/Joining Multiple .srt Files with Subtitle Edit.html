Joining Multiple .srt Files with Subtitle Edit

Since Subtitles don’t end when the movie has reached its length, the addition of Times calculates incorrectly.  

The method should be:  
CHAPTER 1.srt [TIME START = 00:00:00]
plus
CHAPTER 2.srt [TIME START = LENGTH of CHAPTER 1.srt]
...

NOTE:  The subtitles in CHAPTER 2.srt will be start with the beginning of CHAPTER 2.mp4
NOT the ending of TIME SIGNATURES in CHAPTER 1.srt.

If you already have the Chapter Videos and the Chapter SRTs.
This isn’t automatic, but it is a Simple and Fast BRUTE STRENGTH Method.
1) Open Subtitle Workshop.

2) Go to Tools --> Join Subtitles

3) When the new window opens click the ADD button to add your SRT files. Set the frames per second (FPS) of your M2V, AVI, etc.

4) Click JOIN, name your file, and click OK.
