Balsamic Plums and Pineapple Juice Blueberries Compote with Pork Chops and Rice

Ingredients

  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • 6 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 4 cups blueberries
  • A dozen ripe plums, pitted and quartered
  • 3/4 cup pineapple juice

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon ground nutmeg

Directions

  • Pit and Quarter Plums
  • Store in Fridge in Lemon Water over time.
  • Drain off lemon water before cooking
  • Line crock pot with oven bag
  • Layer Prepared Plums and Blueberries
  • In a bowl combine balsamic vinegar, pineapple juice, brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg.
  • Whisk until blended.
  • Pour over the fruit in the crock pot.
  • Crock Pot on High for 60 minutes.  Turn to low for 6 hours.  (Take out early for firm fruit)
  • An hour before presentation begin preparing pork chops.  Turn oven to 400 degrees.
  • Prepare rice.
  • Season Pork Chops with salt and pepper on both sides.
  • Coat a frying pan with olive oil and sear the Pork Chops on Both sides.
  • Line a baking sheet with foil and place the pork chops in the oven for 15 minutes or until your meat thermometer reads at least 160 degrees.  Let pork chops rest for 15 minutes before slicing.
  • Remove 3 cups of the fruit mixture (approximately 1/4 of prepared batch).  Blend until smooth.
  • Layer plate with rice, pork chops, and cover with sauce, with whole fruit mixture on the side.
