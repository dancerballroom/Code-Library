Orange Jell-O Gelatin Pretzel Salad

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon plus 3/4 cup sugar, divided
  • 2 cups finely crushed pretzels
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 packages (3 ounces each) orange gelatin
  • 2 cans (8 ounces each) crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 can (11 ounces) mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cups whipped topping
  • Optional: Additional whipped topping and mandarin oranges

Directions

Crust

  • Preheat oven to 350°. 
  • Mix melted butter and 1 tablespoon sugar; stir in pretzels. 
  • Press onto bottom of an ungreased 13x9-in. baking dish. 
  • Bake 10 minutes. Cool completely on a wire rack.

Jell-O Gelitain

  • In a large bowl, add boiling water to gelatin; stir 2 minutes to completely dissolve. 
  • Stir in fruit. Refrigerate until partially set, about 30 minutes.
  • Meanwhile, in a bowl, beat cream cheese and remaining sugar until smooth. 
  • Fold in whipped topping. Spread over crust.
  • Gently spoon gelatin mixture over top. 
  • Refrigerate, covered, until firm, 2-4 hours. 
  • To serve, cut into squares. 
  • If desired, top with additional whipped topping and oranges.

Editor's Note: 

  • For single-servings, prepare layers as directed. 
  • In each of twelve 9-oz. cups or 1/2-pint canning jars, 
    • layer about 2 Tbsp. pretzel mixture, 
    • 2 Tbsp. cream cheese mixture 
    • and 1/3 cup gelatin mixture. 
  • Refrigerate and top as directed.
