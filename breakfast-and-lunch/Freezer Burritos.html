Freezer Burritos

INGREDIENTS

Veggies

  • 5 bell peppers any color, thinly sliced
  • 1 large yellow onion thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning

Rice

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2.25 cups water

Beef / Meat Filling

  • 2 lbs. 85% ground beef / meat filling
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 5 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • ¾ cup water

Other

  • 8 oz. shredded white cheddar cheese
  • 16 large flour tortillas

INSTRUCTIONS

Veggies and Rice
  • Preheat oven to 375ºF.
  • Thinly slice bell peppers and onion.
  • Place veggies on a large baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
  • Then, season with taco seasoning and toss.
  • Bake at 375ºF for 20-25 minutes or until onions become translucent and set aside.
  • Prepare Rice in Rice Cooker.
Beef / Meat Filling
  • Prepare ground beef / Meat filling.  Sauté for 7-10 minutes.
  • For Ground Beef:  Add in tomato paste, taco seasoning, and water.
  • For Meat Filling:  Heat and set aside.
Preparing the Burritos
  • Lay out all 16 flour tortillas. 
  • Then, evenly distribute meat, veggies, and rice. 
  • Sprinkle on a handful of cheese.
  • Wrap each burrito by folding the sides in and rolling.
  • Wrap each individual burrito in foil (remove as much air as possible)

Storage

  • Store in the freezer for up to 3 months.
