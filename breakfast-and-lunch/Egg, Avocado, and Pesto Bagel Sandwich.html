Egg, Avocado, and Pesto Bagel Sandwich

Ingredients

  •  1 Bagel, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon Pesto
  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 slice Havarti cheese
  • 1/2 cup arugula
  • 1/2 of avocado sliced


Instructions

  • Spread the pesto on the inside of both bagel halves. Set aside.
  • In a small bowl or mug, scramble the two eggs together with a fork.
  • Season with salt and pepper, to taste. S
  • pray a small skillet with nonstick cooking spray and pour the eggs in the pan.
  • Heat over medium-low heat and cook until just set.
  • Flip and continue to cook until egg is cooked.
  • Place the slice of cheese on top of the egg and turn off the heat.
  • Let the egg sit in the pan for a second so the cheese can melt.
  • Place some arugula on the bottom half of the bagel and add the cheesy egg on top.
  • Add the avocado slices.
  • Season with salt and pepper, to taste, and add the rest of the arugula.
  • Top with the other bagel half.
  • Serve immediately.
