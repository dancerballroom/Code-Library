Classic Patty Melt

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 8 ounces 90/10 ground beef
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 slices sandwich bread
  • 4 slices Swiss cheese
  • Mustard, optional

Directions

  • Cook 1 tablespoon of butter and sliced onions for about 25 minutes.
  • At 20 minutes stir in apple cider vinegar. 
Meanwhile: 
  • Shape ground beef into thin 4-ounce patties the shape of your bread. 
  • Season patties with salt and pepper.
  • When onions are done in your skillet, set the heat to medium-high and add the patties. 
  • Cook for 3 minutes per side. 
  • Sautee one side of each slice of bread with butter. 
  • Remove half of the bread and start building your sandwich in the skillet.
  • On each slice of bread: 
    • Add a slice of Swiss cheese, 
    • A patty, 
    • Half of the caramelized onions
    • Top with the second slice of Swiss cheese. 
    • Top with the slice of bread removed buttered side up.
  • Over medium-low heat, cook the sandwiches for 3-4 minutes per side 
    • until cheese melted and bread is toasted.
