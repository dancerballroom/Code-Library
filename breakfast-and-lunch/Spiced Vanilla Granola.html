Spiced Vanilla Granola

  • Can be made 2 weeks ahead.  Store in an airtight container at room temp.
  • Serve with fresh fruit and yogurt, or topping for muffins, or plain.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 cup chopped pecans
  • 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • Optional:  Sunflower Seeds, Sesame Seeds, Coconut, Flax Seeds

Directions

  • Preheat oven 300 degrees F.
  • Grease large baking pan.
  • In large bowl mix oats, pecans, brown sugar, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  • In small sauce pan combine the oil maple syrup, and granulated sugar.
  • Bring to simmer over medium heat.
  • Remove from the heat and still in the vanilla.
  • Pour over hot oat mixture, stir well.
  • Using your hands toss the mixture until thoroughly combined.
  • Spread in the prepared baking pan.
  • Bake, stirring occasionally for about 30 minutes, or until golden brown.
  • Transfer the pan to a cooling rack until cooled completely.

Note: I stir it every 15 minutes
Note: I cook it a little longer and then I turn off the oven and let it cool in the oven.
