Spam Fried Rice

Goes great together with Chinese Hot and Sour Soup.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons vegetable oil divided use
  • 2 cans Spam cut into 1/2 inch squares
  • 1 cup yellow onion chopped
  • 4 carrots peeled, quartered and sliced
  • 1 cup frozen peas thawed
  • 1 heaping Tablespoon minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoons minced ginger
  • 4 eggs lightly beaten
  • 3 cups cooked long grain white rice 
    • leftover rice works best
  • 6 tablespoons soy sauce low sodium
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup green onions thinly sliced

Directions

  • Heat 2 teaspoons of oil in a large pan over medium high heat.
  • Place the Spam squares in a single layer in the pan. 
    • Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, or until meat is browned.
  • Remove the Spam from the pan. Place it on a plate then cover with foil to keep warm.
  • Pour 1 teaspoon of oil into the pan, then add the onion and carrots. 
    • Cook for 4-5 minutes or until veggies are softened. 
    • Season the vegetables with salt and pepper.
  • Add the garlic and ginger, then cook for 30 seconds.
  • Remove the vegetables from the pan. 
    • Place them on the plate with the Spam and cover with foil to keep warm.
  • Pour the remaining teaspoon of oil into the pan. 
    • Add the eggs and cook, 
      • stirring occasionally and breaking up with a spatula, 
      • until the eggs are scrambled. 
    • Add a bit of salt and pepper to the eggs.
  • Add the rice, Spam, cooked veggies and peas into the pan.
  • Stir in the soy sauce and sesame oil and mix 
    • until everything is thoroughly combined
