Dragon Fingers (1.5 hrs with prep)

Ingredients

  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 16 oz. (or half a 32 oz.)  shredded cheddar jack
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 12 medium jalapeño peppers, stems removed, halved lengthwise and seeded
  • 12 shrimp prawns, cooked, deveined
  • 1 package bacon strips
  • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

Directions

Prep:
Cut off the 16 jalapeños stems and cut down the middle.  These will be 2 boats for roux and shrimp.  Remove all the seeds.  Set aside in fridge.  Remove the hard part of the prawn.  Set aside in fridge.  Cook the bacon into small pieces until crisp.

Creating the Roux:
  • In a saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat. 
  • Add in chili powder and garlic powder.
  • Stir in enough flour to make a roux.   
  • Add milk to roux slowly, stirring constantly. 
  • Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and the sauce is a little thick.  Add in cooked bacon.
  • Set aside and let cool.
Assembling the Dragon Finger before baking:
  • Spoon about 1 tablespoonful of the roux mixture into each half of the cut peppers. 
  • Dip tops of stuffed peppers into bread crumbs.
  • Place on a baking sheet.  Bake at 325° for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
  • Let sit 10 minutes.
