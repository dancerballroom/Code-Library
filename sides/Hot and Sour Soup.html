Hot and Sour Soup

Serve with: Spam Fried Rice or see What to Serve with Fried Rice

INGREDIENTS

1st 7 ingredients

  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 8 ounces baby bella mushrooms)
    • thinly-sliced with stems discarded
  • 1 (8-ounce) can bamboo shoots, drained
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar, or more
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon chili garlic sauce

Thickener

  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 large eggs, whisked
  • 8 ounces firm tofu*, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • Kosher salt and black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Set aside ¼ cup of the chicken or vegetable broth for later use.
  • Add the 1st 7 Ingredients to a large stock pot, and stir to combine. 
  • Heat over medium-high heat until the soup reaches a simmer.
  • While the soup is heating, whisk together the ¼ cup of broth 
    • and cornstarch in a small bowl until completely smooth. 
    • Once the soup has reached a simmer, 
      • stir in the cornstarch mixture and stir for 1 minute
        • until the soup has thickened.
  • Stir Soup, Drizzle in whisked eggs in a thin stream to create egg ribbons. 
  • Stir in the tofu, half of the green onions, and sesame oil. 
  • Season the soup with salt and a pinch* of white/black pepper to taste. 
    • NOTE: For a more “sour” soup, add in 1 or 2 tablespoons of rice wine vinegar.
    • NOTE: For a “spicier” soup, add in more chili garlic sauce.
  • Serve immediately, garnished with the extra green onions.
