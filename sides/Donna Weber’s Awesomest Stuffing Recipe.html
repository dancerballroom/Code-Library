Donna Weber’s Awesomest Stuffing Recipe

Ingredients

  • Mrs. Cubbison's Stuffing Mix, Cubed and Traditional
  • 1/4 lb. Ground Sirloin, or lean Ground Beef, increase amount if no Veal
  • 1/4 lb. Pork Sausage, Jimmy Dean, increase amount if no Veal.
  • (optional) 1/4 lb. Veal
  • Medium Yellow Onion
  • Turkey Juice / Chicken Broth
  • 2 or 3 Eggs, RAW
  • 1/2 Can Cream of Celery Soup
  • 1/2 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup

Directions

  • Cook Ground Beef, finely chopped, and drain oil.
  • Cook Sausage
  • Cook Veal (if available)
  • Sauté Onion
  • Mix it all together.
  • Preferably, use Turkey juice, either basting 
    • or the juice from boiling the neck, heart, and liver.
    • or Chicken Broth.
  • Mix it together
  • Stuff the Turkey, or Bake in baking pan 45 minutes, 
    • covered with foil at 350 degrees.
  • Take foil off and brown for 15 minutes.
  • Feed the world!
