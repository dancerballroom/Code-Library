Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies

with Chocolate Chips

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light or dark brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups old-fashioned whole rolled oats
  • 2 and 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  • Whisk the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl. Set aside.
  • beat the butter on medium-high speed until smooth, about 1 minute. 
  • Add sugar and brown sugar and beat about 2 minutes. 
  • Add the eggs, peanut butter, and vanilla and beat about 1 minute.
  • Mix dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix. 
  • Add the oats. 
  • Add in the chocolate chips. Dough will be thick and sticky. 
  • Cover and chill the dough for at least 20 minutes in the refrigerator. 
  • Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C). 
  • Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. Set aside.
  • Scoop balls of dough, 2 Tablespoons of dough per cookie, 
    • arrange 3 inches apart on the baking sheets. 
  • Bake for 12-14 minutes until lightly browned on the sides.
  • Remove from the oven. Let sit for 5 minutes.
