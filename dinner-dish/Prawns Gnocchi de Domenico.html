Prawns Gnocchi de Domenico

Makes 5 (1 cup) Servings
  • 6               oz.                    Gnocchi
  • 6               tblspoons     Butter
  • 2               tblspoons     Flour
  • 1 1/2         cups             Milk
  • 1/4            tspoon         Salt
  • 1/8            tspoon         Pepper
  • 1/4            cup                White Wine
  • 1               Jar (2 oz.)      Pimientos, Sliced
  • 1/2            cup                Scallions, Sliced
  • 1/4            lb.                  Sliced Mushrooms
  • 1/2            cup                Bell Pepper, thinly sliced (1in. long)
  • 1               tspoon            Garlic, minced
  • 1/2            lb.                  Raw Prawns or Shrimp
  • 1               tblspoon        Lemon Juice
  • 1/4            cup                Parsley, chopped
  • Cook 6 oz. gnocchi, and drain.
  • Melt 2 tblspoons Butter, whisk in 2 tblspoons flour.
  • Cook 10 minutes over low heat.
  • Stir in 1 1/2 cups Milk, 1/4 tspoon Salt, 1/8 tspoon pepper.
  • Cook and stir until slightly thickened.
  • Stir in 1/4 cup white wine and 1 jar (2 oz.) sliced pimientos  and remove from heat.
  • In frying pan, sauté 1/2 cup sliced Scallions, 1/4 lb. sliced mushrooms, 1/2 cup thinly sliced Bell pepper (1 in. long) and 1 tspoon minced garlic in 4 tblspoons butter for 2 minutes.
  • Add the prawns and set aside to infuse.
  • Add sauce and simmer covered for 5 minutes.
  • Stir in 1 tblspoon lemon juice and 1/4 cup chopped parsley.10.   
  • Serve over gnocchi.
