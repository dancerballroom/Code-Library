Skillet Apple Crisp

In Two Parts:  

  • Topping 
  • Fruit Prep/Serving

Ingredients:

  • 6 tablespoons (3/4 Stock) Butter
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Walnuts or Pecans
  • 1 tablespoon Grated Lemon Zest
  • 1/2 cup Rolled Oats
  • 1/4 cup Shredded Unsweetened Coconut
  • 1/3 cup Packed Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon
  • pinch Salt

The Topping

  • Put 5 tablespoons butter in large skillet, Cover low heat.
  • When it's melted, add 
    • nuts, 
    • lemon zest, 
    • oats, 
    • coconut, 
    • packed brown sugar, 
    • cinnamon, and a 
    • pinch of salt.  
    • Toss to coat.
  • Cook, stirring frequently, until topping is golden, and crisp from 6 to 8 minutes.
  • Remove from the pan, no need to wipe it out.

Part 2:  The Fruit

  • Put 1 tablespoon butter in the skillet over medium heat.
  • When it's melted, add fruit and cook stirring occasionally until fruit is soft but not mushy, 5 to 6 minutes.
  • Scatter the topping over the warm fruit and serve.     

NOTE:  This recipe can be made with any fruit you like:

  • Berries, 
  • Apples, and 
  • Mangoes.
  • Adjust cooking time based on the firmness of the fruit.
