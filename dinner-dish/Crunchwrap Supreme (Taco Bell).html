Crunchwrap Supreme (Taco Bell)

INGREDIENTS

  • Refried Beans optional
  • 4 lb. ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • 16 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cans Nacho Cheese Sauce
  • 16 tostada shells
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1 big bag of Shredded Napa Cabbage
  • 4 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 16 oz. shredded Cheddar
  • Green Onions
  • 16 oz. Shredded Monterey Jack
  • Vegetable oil
  • Hot sauce, for serving
  • 3 empty ice cream buckets for storage in the refrigerator

DIRECTIONS

  • In a skillet over medium heat, add ground beef, AND taco seasoning 5 minutes. 
  • Drain fat, then season with salt and pepper.
  • Cut small flour tortillas: 
  • Stack four large flour tortillas and place a tostada shell in the center. 
  • Trace around the edges of the shell to cut four smaller flour tortilla rounds. Set aside.
  • Build crunchwraps: 
    • ground beef to the center of each tortilla
    • Drizzle cheese sauce over each, 
    • place tostada shells on top. 
    • Spread sour cream over each shell 
    • Top with lettuce, green onion, tomato, and cheeses.
  • Place the smaller flour tortilla cutouts in the center of each crunchwrap
  • tightly fold the edges of the large tortilla towards the center, creating pleats. 
  • After wrapping, quickly invert crunchwraps, pleats are on the bottom staying together.
  • Cook crunchwraps:  In a medium nonstick pan over medium heat with vegetable oil. 
    • Add crunchwrap seam-side down and cook until tortilla is golden, 3 to 5 minutes. 
    • Flip crunchwrap and cook until the other side is golden, 3 to 5 minutes more.
  • Repeat with remaining crunchwraps and serve warm with hot sauce.
