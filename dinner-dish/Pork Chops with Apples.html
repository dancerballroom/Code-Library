Pork Chops with Apples

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 (1-inch thick) boneless center cut pork chops
  • 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 Half Stick (1/4 cup) Butter with Olive Oil & Sea Salt
  • 1 large (1 1/2 cups) onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh garlic
  • 2 apples, cored, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley, if desired

DIRECTIONS

  • Sprinkle both sides of pork chops with salt and pepper.
  • Melt Butter with Olive Oil & Sea Salt in 12-inch skillet over high heat until sizzling.
  • Add pork chops; cook, turning once, 6-8 minutes or until pork is browned.
  • Remove pork chops from pan; keep warm.  Reduce heat to medium; add onions and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally, 1-2 minutes or until onions begin to soften.
  • Add apples and lemon juice; continue cooking 3-4 minutes or until apples just begin to soften.
  • Reduce heat to low; return pork chops to pan.
  • Cover; cook 6-10 minutes or until pork reaches an internal temperature of 145°F. and is no longer pink.
  • Remove pork chops from pan; keep warm.
  • Add maple syrup to pan.
  • Increase heat to medium-high; cook, stirring occasionally, 5-7 min. until sauce comes to a boil and thickens.
  • Place apple mixture onto serving platter.   Top with pork chops; sprinkle with chopped parsley, if desired.
