Cornbread Ground Beef Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup chopped onion 
  • 1 pound of ground beef 
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder 
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground pepper
  • ½ teaspoon paprika 
  • 1 can (10.5 oz.) of cream of chicken
  • 1 can (10 oz.) of diced tomatoes with green chilis
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheese, divided 
  • 2 boxes (8.5 oz. each) Corn Muffin Mix
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  • Butter a 9 x 13 bake dish
  • In a pan add about 1 teaspoon of olive oil 
  • Cook the onions until they are translucent. 
  • Mix the ground beef and the seasoning 
  • Break the meat into pieces.
  • Add the cream of chicken 
  • Add diced tomatoes, 
  • And sour cream and 1 cup Colby Jack cheese.
  • Mix until all blended. Set aside
  • In a large mixing bowl, add 
    • Two boxes of Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix, 
    • and add milk and eggs. 
  • Whisk until all combined.
  • Spray Pyrex (9 x 13.) with Butter/Oil
  • Pour in the cornbread mixture
  • Add a spoonful of the meat mixture  Don’t mix.
  • Spread the 1 cup shredded cheese on top.
  • 350 degrees F. for 30-35 minutes.
    • Insert a knife to test if done..
  • Serve and Enjoy!
