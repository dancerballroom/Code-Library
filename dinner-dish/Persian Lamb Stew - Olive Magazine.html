Persian Lamb Stew - Olive Magazine

Ingredients

  • oil for frying
  • onions 2, chopped
  • red chilli 1, seeded and diced
  • garlic 2 cloves, crushed
  • lamb leg steaks 2 (about 450 g), fat trimmed and cut into chunks
  • ground cinnamon 1 tsp
  • ground cumin 1 tsp
  • cardamom 3 pods, bashed
  • saffron a pinch or ½ tsp turmeric
  • chicken stock 400 ml
  • tomato purée 1 tbsp
  • pomegranate molasses 1 tbsp
  • chickpeas 400 g tin, rinsed and drained
  • mint or coriander a small bunch, chopped
  • pomegranate seeds 4 tbsp
  • cucumber ½, seeded and sliced

Directions

  • Heat 1 tsp oil in a casserole, and fry the onions with a pinch of salt for 5 minutes until soft.
  • Add the chilli and garlic for a minute.
  • Add the lamb and cook for 5 minutes until brown.
  • Stir in the spices.
  • Fry for a minute, then add the stock, purée and molasses. Cover with a lid.
  • Cook for 40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add the chickpeas, season
  • Cook for another 30 minutes until the lamb is tender.
  • Scatter some herbs and pomegranate seeds over the lamb.
  • Serve with the remaining pomegranate tossed with the cucumber.
