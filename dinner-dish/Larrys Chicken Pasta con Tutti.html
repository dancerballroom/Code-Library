Larry's Chicken Pasta con Tutti

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces Penne Pasta 
  • 2 tablespoons Extra-virgin Olive oil 
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless Chicken breast or thighs, 
    • trimmed, cut into bite-size pieces 
  • Pinch Salt
  • Pinch ground Pepper
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • ½ cup dry white Wine
  • Juice and zest of 1 Lemon

Tutti Section

  • 2 Cups Fresh Spinach
  • 1 can White Corn
  • 1 15 oz. can Sliced Mushrooms
  • 1 15 oz. can Stewed/Diced Tomatoes
  • 2 Tablespoons melted Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Parmesan cheese

Directions

  • Cook pasta according to package in a large Pot. Drain. Set aside.
  • Meanwhile, heat oil in a large high-sided skillet over medium-high heat. 
  • Add chicken, salt and pepper; cook, stirring occasionally, 
    • until just cooked through, 5 to 7 minutes. 
  • Add garlic and cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 1 minute. 
  • Stir in wine, lemon juice and zest; bring to a simmer.
  • Add melted Butter to Pasta, add Parmesan Cheese
  • Stir in all items from Tutti Section.
  • Cook until well blended. 
  • Add to the Pasta Pot.  Serve and Store.
