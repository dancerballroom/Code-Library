Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients

For the potatoes:

  • 1 1/2 pounds russet potatoes
  • 1/4 cup half-and-half
  • 2 ounces unsalted butter

For the meat filling:

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced small
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground lamb or chicken or turkey
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen English peas
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 egg yolk

  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons freshly chopped rosemary leaves
  • 1 teaspoon freshly chopped thyme leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen English peas

Instructions

  • Boil the potatoes.  Bring water to a boil, reduce to a simmer, boil 20 minutes.
  • Sauté carrots and onions about 6 minutes.
  • Add peas and corn about 4 minutes.
  • Add ground beef and cook until no longer pink.
  • Add salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce and beef broth.
  • Cook low 10 minutes adding beef broth if dry.
  • Mash cooked potatoes.
  • Layer the meat mixture and mashed potatoes in a casserole dish.
  • Cook 400°F until browned and bubbling, about 30 minutes.
Suggested variations:
Put a layer of creamed corn between the ground beef and the mashed potatoes.
Sprinkle grated cheddar cheese over the top of the mashed potatoes before baking.
