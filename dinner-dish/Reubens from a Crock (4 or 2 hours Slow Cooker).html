Reubens from a Crock (4 or 2 hours Slow Cooker)

Ingredients

  • 1   2- to 3- lb. corned beef brisket with spice packet.
  • 16-oz. jar sauerkraut, drained
  • 1/2 cup bottled Thousand Island salad dressing
  • 16 slices rye swirl bread, toasted
  • 8 oz. Swiss cheese, sliced
  • Bottled Thousand Island salad dressing

Directions

  • Trim fat from meat.  If necessary, cut meat to fit in a 3 1/2- or 4-quart slow cooker.  Place meat in slow cooker.  Sprinkle spices from packet evenly over meat; rub in with your fingers.  Spread sauerkraut over meat.  Drizzle the 1/2 cup salad dressing over all.
  • Cover and cook on low heat setting for 4 to 6 hours or on high heat setting for 2 to 3 hours.
  • Transfer meat to a cutting board.  Thinly slice meat across the grain.  Return sliced meat to the slow cooker and stir to combine with the cooking juices.  Using a slotted spoon, spoon meat mixture onto 8 slices of toasted bread.  Top with cheese.  If desired, top with additional salad dressing.  Top with remaining bread.
