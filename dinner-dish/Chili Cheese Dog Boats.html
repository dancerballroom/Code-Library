Chili Cheese Dog Boats

Ingredients (for 8 servings)

  • 8 hot dog buns
  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped, plus more for garnish
  • 8 slices cheddar cheese
  • 15 oz chili, with or without beans
  • 8 hot dogs

Preparation

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  • Leaving attached, place the 8 hot dog buns in a 9 x 13-inch baking dish.
  • Cut a rectangle in each hot dog bun, 1 centimeter from the edges. 
  • Push down on the bread cut-out to compact it tightly into the bottom of each bun
  • Press down on the bottom edges as well.
  • Mix the butter, garlic, and parsley in a small bowl.
  • Brush the buns and their hollowed insides with the garlic butter. 
  • Bake for 5 minutes to toast the buns.
  • Place a slice of cheddar cheese inside each bun.
  • Spoon a dollop of chili inside each one. 
  • Slide a hot dog snugly on top of the chili in each bun.
  • Top with more chili.
  • Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the cheese is golden brown. 
  • Top with fresh parsley, 
  • Slice the hot dog boats along their connected seams to serve.
  • Enjoy!
