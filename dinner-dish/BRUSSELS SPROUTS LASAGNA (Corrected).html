BRUSSELS SPROUTS LASAGNA (Corrected)

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 package lasagna 
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, stems removed and sliced
  • 1 green onion, diced
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 12 ounces sliced cremini mushrooms
  • 12 ounces shiitake mushrooms, stems removed
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 3/4 cups milk, 2% or whole will work best
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 8 ounces provolone cheese, grated
  • 3/4 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Romano cheese
INSTRUCTIONS
  • Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Spray a 9×13 baking dish with nonstick spray.
  • Cook the lasagna according to the instructions on its package.
  • Add the Brussels, shallots and 1/4 teaspoon each of the salt and pepper, stirring to coat.
  • Cook until the sprouts soften and become bright in color, stirring occasionally, for about 5 to 6 minutes.
  • Stir in the balsamic vinegar and cook for another minute and then set aside
  • Using the same skillet, add the remaining olive oil add the mushrooms and garlic, stirring well to coat. 
  • Cook until the mushrooms are soft and juicy, stirring occasionally, about 6 to 8 minutes.
  • Turn off the heat and add 1/4 teaspoon each of salt and pepper.
  • In a saucepan, heat the butter over medium heat and whisk in the flour to create a roux. 
  • Cook the roux for 2 or 3 minutes, until it becomes golden and fragrant.
  • Add milk, stirring constantly, until it starts to thicken, about 5 minutes. 
  • Season the mixture with the remaining salt, pepper and nutmeg.
  • Assemble the lasagna:
  • Add 1/4 of the sauce to the bottom of the 9×13 dish. 
  • Add a layer or lasagna sheets, then top with 1/3 brussels sprouts and 1/3 of mushrooms. 
  • Sprinkle on 1/3 cup cheese and sprinkle on 1/4 cup of the ricotta 
  • Repeat 1/4 sauce, Lasagna sheets, 1/3 brussels sprouts, 1/3 mushrooms
  • Finish with remaining cheese, the Romano cheese and remaining sauce.
  • Bake for 30 to 35 minutes at 400 degrees until the top is golden. 
  • Let sit for 20 minutes.
