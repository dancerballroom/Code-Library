Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. lean Beef, boneless round steak, about 1/2 inch thick, cut in 1/2 inch squares.
  • 1 tablespoon margarine (ersatz: butter), low calorie
  • 1/2  cup Onion, chopped
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 3 cups Sliced mushrooms (about 8 oz.)
  • 1/4 cup Red Wine, dry
  • 2 tablespoons Cornstarch
  • 1 cup Condensed Beef Broth (ersatz: Broth from Crock Pot)
  • 1/4 teaspoon Pepper
  • 3/4 cup Sour Cream
  • 2 cups cooked Egg Noodles or Rice
  • 2 tablespoons Parsley, snipped

Directions

  •  Sauté onion and garlic in sauce pan.
  •  Stir in beef and mushrooms.
  • Add wine, simmer 10 minutes.
  • Stir in Corn Starch,  Cook and stir until thickened.
  • No heat.  
  • Stir in Pepper and Sour Cream.  Low heat.
  • Add Egg Noodles
  • Simmer stirring occasionally, about 30 minutes.
  • Serve over noodles and sprinkle with parsley.
