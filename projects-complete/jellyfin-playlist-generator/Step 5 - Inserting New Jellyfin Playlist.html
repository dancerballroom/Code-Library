Step 5 - Inserting New Jellyfin Playlist

Purpose:  Generate Playlist on the fly integrating multiple TV Show Series (In Order)
Programlette Idea : Creating the Insertable Code for Generated Playlist (Series)
Parts of 10s:
  • Jellyfin Playlists (Data Folder) Location:  
    • C:\ProgramData\Jellyfin\Server\data\playlists
      • Animation Unseen FOLDER/PLAYLIST
      • Best Animation FOLDER/PLAYLIST
      • Best of Classics FOLDER/PLAYLIST
      • July 2023 FOLDER/PLAYLIST
      • Watch, Wait, Repeat FOLDER/PLAYLIST
      • Etc.
  • playlist.xml in each folder
    • Create Generic Playlist inside of Jellyfin, Adding One Movie (Example: Blazing Saddles)
    • Find Playlist in C:\ProgramData\Jellyfin\Server\data\playlists\Generic 
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8" standalone="yes"?>
<Item>
  <ContentRating>R</ContentRating>
  <Added>07/11/2023 08:34:06</Added>
  <LockData>false</LockData>
  <LocalTitle>Generic</LocalTitle>
  <RunningTime>93</RunningTime>
  <Genres>
    <Genre>Western</Genre>
    <Genre>Comedy</Genre>
  </Genres>
  <Studios>
    <Studio>Crossbow Productions</Studio>
    <Studio>Warner Bros. Pictures</Studio>
  </Studios>
  <PlaylistItems>
    <PlaylistItem>
      <Path>F:\COMEDY Movies A-M\Blazing Saddles_.mp4</Path>
    </PlaylistItem>
  </PlaylistItems>
  <Shares>
    <Share>
      <UserId>533876103b9f41f8b1e695a313abb409</UserId>
      <CanEdit>true</CanEdit>
    </Share>
  </Shares>
  <PlaylistMediaType>Audio</PlaylistMediaType>
</Item>
  • The PlaylistItem Section is the one that needs to be Generated by the Program.
  • After Pasting/Saving in the Generated XML, Refresh the MetaData Playlist and Voila!
